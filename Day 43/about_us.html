About us

At Lulini Store, we believe that fashion is more than just clothing; it's a form of self-expression, a way to showcase individuality, and a celebration of personal style. Our mission is to bring you a curated collection of high-quality, stylish, and affordable clothing that reflects the latest trends while staying true to timeless elegance.

Our Story
Founded in 2024, Lulini Store emerged from a passion for fashion and a desire to create a unique shopping experience. Our founder, Luka Tskhvaradze, envisioned a place where people could find clothes that make them feel confident, comfortable, and chic, no matter the occasion. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a beloved destination for fashion enthusiasts who appreciate quality and style.

Our collections
At Lulini Store, we offer a diverse range of clothing and accessories for every style and occasion. Whether you're looking for casual wear, office attire, evening gowns, or statement pieces, we have something to suit your needs. Our collections are thoughtfully designed and curated to ensure that every item you find in our store meets the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Our commitment
We are committed to providing our customers with exceptional service and a seamless shopping experience. From the moment you enter our store or visit our website, we strive to make your journey with us enjoyable and satisfying. Our team is always here to assist you, offering personalized styling advice and helping you find the perfect pieces to complete your wardrobe.

Sustainability
At Lulini Store, we care about the environment and our community. We are dedicated to promoting sustainable fashion by sourcing materials responsibly and supporting ethical manufacturing practices. Our goal is to minimize our environmental footprint while delivering beautiful, long-lasting clothing.
